What is Ethics and Why is it Important?

Ethics is the set of principles that guide our decisions and actions, ensuring we act in a way that is fair, just, and beneficial to society. In engineering, ethics ensures public safety, promotes sustainability, and fosters trust between engineers and the communities they serve.
